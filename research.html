My Research

Potholes Detection using Advanced Object Detection Techniques (Ongoing)

Abstract: Developed a real-time pothole detection system using an ensemble of YOLOv8m and MobileNetV2 models, achieving 90.3% accuracy across challenging conditions like low-light, rain, and cluttered environments. Integrated advanced preprocessing (CycleGAN, CLAHE), model optimization and edge deployment for scalable smart road monitoring.
